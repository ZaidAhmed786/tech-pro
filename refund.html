Service Cancellation Rights

You have the right to cancel any service Order within 7 days of the booking date, provided that work has not commenced. Once the service delivery has started, cancellation eligibility is determined based on the stage of completion.

To exercise your cancellation right, please inform us via:

  • Email: info@shahlani3@yahoo.com

Upon approval, any eligible refund will be processed within 14 days using the original payment method. No additional charges will be applied for the reimbursement.

Conditions for Refunds

To be eligible for a refund:

  • The service must have been booked within the last 7 days.
  • Work on the service must not be substantially completed.
  • A clear reason for dissatisfaction must be provided, subject to review.

Non-Refundable Services Include:

  • Services that have been fully delivered or completed.
  • Customized or tailor-made services already initiated based on your specific requirements.
  • Services involving third-party tools or subscriptions already purchased on your behalf.
  • Any service purchased at a discounted or promotional price (unless required by applicable law).

We reserve the right to decline a refund request if the above conditions are not met.

Service Modifications or Adjustments

If you are dissatisfied with the quality of service delivered, we encourage you to contact us for a revision or adjustment before requesting a refund. We are committed to ensuring customer satisfaction and will make reasonable efforts to address your concerns.

Gifts and Promotional Services

If the service was marked as a gift when purchased and it was delivered directly to you, you may receive a credit or voucher in the form of service value. Otherwise, refunds will be issued to the original purchaser.
